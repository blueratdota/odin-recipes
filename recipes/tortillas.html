Pink Guy's Forbidden Tortillas Recipe

  1. Buy the tortilla wrap from some wet store
  2. Go to the back of the wet store's building and start scavenging from the trash bins
  3. Gather some dead rats. Preferrably not more than a week dead
  4. Boil the rat to clean it off
  5. Wrap it with the tortilla wrap
  6. Enjoy!/s Please don't actually do this.
